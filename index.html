HarmonicShift Quick Guide

1. Input: Type or paste standard chord names (like C Am F G or Dm7 G7 Cmaj7) into the text box. Separate them with spaces, commas, or line breaks.

2. Transpose: Click the - and + buttons to instantly shift notes up or down by semitones.

3. Sound Engine: Tap any individual chord badge to play it out loud. Use the Sequencer Bar at the bottom of the input panel to play the entire progression as a loop.

4. Theory Deep-Dive: The right dashboard breaks down every chord, displays all unique notes (sorted dynamically starting from the root of your first chord), and checks standard Western modes and Hindustani classical Thaats matching your progression.
